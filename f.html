USES

Cereal Packaging


PP bags are the preferred choice for rice,wheat etc., delivering benefits like freshness preservation, moisture resistance, and cost-effectiveness.They help extend the shelf life of rice and flour by protecting against external elements and contaminants.

Spice Packaging


PP bags provide an excellent barrier against moisture and air, ensuring spices stay fresh and flavorful for an extended period. Available in various sizes and styles, PP bags can be tailored to suit different spice packaging needs.

Flour and Sugar Packaging


PP bags provide an excellent barrier against moisture, keeping flour and sugar dry and preventing clumping or spoilage.PP bags are suitable for packaging a wide range of flour and sugar products, from refined sugar to specialty flours, making them versatile for the food industry.
